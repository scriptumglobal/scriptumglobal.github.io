Sebastian O2

The Sebastian O2, developed by Aquila Core AI, is a next-generation autonomous quadruped robot designed for real-world operations across diverse terrains. Equipped with advanced AI navigation, real-time obstacle avoidance, and precision control, Sebastian O2 offers unmatched versatility for applications such as security patrols, industrial inspections, hazardous environment exploration, research missions, and search & rescue. Its modular design allows for seamless integration of payloads including cameras, thermal sensors, LiDAR, and communication systems, enabling full customization for mission-specific requirements. Whether operating autonomously or via remote control, Sebastian O2 delivers reliable ground-based autonomy where human presence may be difficult, dangerous, or impossible.

SPECCOM Drones

The SpecCom Drones by Special Communications Aquila Limited are advanced AI-powered long-range UAV platforms built for extended missions in the most demanding environments. Featuring long-endurance flight capability, high payload capacity, and real-time onboard AI processing, these drones provide actionable aerial intelligence for industrial inspections, border surveillance, infrastructure monitoring, precision agriculture, disaster response, mapping, and search & rescue. With integrated advanced sensors such as thermal imaging, LiDAR, multispectral cameras, and HD video, SpecCom Drones deliver high-resolution data in real-time while operating safely at scale. Designed for both autonomous and operator-directed missions, SpecCom Drones bring unmatched operational reach, data accuracy, and mission flexibility to the aerial domain.
